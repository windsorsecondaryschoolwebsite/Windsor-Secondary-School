School Rules

Key standards expected of all Windsor learners

Behaviour & Conduct
  • 01Attend school daily and arrive punctually
  • 02Respect all staff, learners and school property
  • 03No cell phones during school hours
  • 04No violence, bullying or intimidation
  • 05Complete all homework and assignments on time
Uniform & Appearance
  • 01Wear full prescribed monthly uniform daily
  • 02No hair colour, dye, extensions or fancy accessories
  • 03No jewellery except a wristwatch
  • 04Short, neat nails — no nail polish
  • 05No make-up or colour contact lenses

Fees & Banking

School fees, banking details and general information

School Fees

The compulsory school fees for 2025 were R2 500 per learner per annum and may increase in 2026 to R2 800.00.

Payment must be done via bank deposit or EFT — no cash will be accepted at school. Proof of payment must be submitted to the school. Please ensure that the correct reference details are used when making payment.
